Yasin Shuman

Full Stack Developer

Thanks for checking out my page. I'm a junior full stack developer currently attending Lambda School's Computer Science Program. I'm looking forward to sharing my journey of becoming a better developer and person with you. You can follow my progress by reading my blog posts on Medium.

Outside of work, I volunteer at least once a month with groups such as #rake (Random Acts of Kindness Everywhere) as well as having participated in #hashtaglunchbag events in Louisville, Cleveland and San Francisco. I have always felt strongly about social issues and helping my neighbors. In my previous career, I worked at eliminating food deserts in Pittsburgh.
